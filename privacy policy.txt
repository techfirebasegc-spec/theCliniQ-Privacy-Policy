
Privacy Policy – The CliniQ

Effective Date: 24-03-2026
App Name: The CliniQ

The CliniQ (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides teleconsultation, chat consultation, and home visit healthcare services connecting users with doctors and physiotherapists.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently in compliance with Google Play User Data Policy.

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services.

- Name

- Address (for home visits)

b. Sensitive Health Information

- Medical history
- Symptoms and consultation details
- Prescriptions and reports

⚠️ This data is considered sensitive personal data and is handled with strict confidentiality.

c. Payment Information

- Payments are processed via secure third-party providers (e.g., Razorpay, Stripe)
- We do not store debit/credit card details

d. Device & Usage Data
